Interested in Sponsoring NCCB 2017?

The Northern California Computational Biology (NCCB) Student Symposium is a great event to access the computational biology student community and to promote your organization directly to conference attendees. Please consider partnering with us in sponsoring this important scientific and educational event.

Sponsorship Levels

We are requesting sponsorship at the following levels. All sponsor representatives are welcome to attend all symposium talks, poster session and reception.

Gold Level ($2,000+)
  • Conference passes for up to four representatives to attend symposium
  • Opportunity for roundtable meetings with students
  • Access to resumes from opt-in attendees
  • Tables to use to exhibit your materials
  • Distribution of promotional materials to all attendees
  • Corporate name and logo will be displayed on NCCB website and printed materials
Silver Level ($1,000+)
  • Conference passes for up to two representatives to attend symposium
  • Access to resumes from opt-in attendees
  • Table to use to exhibit your materials
  • Distribution of promotional materials to all attendees
  • Corporate name and logo will be displayed on NCCB website and printed materials
Bronze Level ($500+)
  • Conference pass for a representative to attend the symposium
  • Shared table to use to exhibit your materials
  • Corporate name and logo will be displayed on NCCB website and printed materials

Other Sponsorship Opportunities

If you are interested in supporting the NCCB Student Symposium but would like to inquire about sponsoring the event in other ways, such as providing a smaller amount of donation for coffee, lunch, t-shirts, or providing other giveaways to conference attendees, please feel free to contact us at: nccbsymposium@gmail.com.
