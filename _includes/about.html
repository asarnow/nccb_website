About

NCCB is the Northern California Computational Biology student group for PhD, masters, and undergraduate students based in Northern California.

Students from UC Berkeley, UC Santa Cruz, UCSF, and Stanford NCCB produce a one-day symposium of student and industry talks, a poster session, and networking opportunities that encompass all fields related to computational biology.

The 2017 NCCB symposium will take place on Saturday, October 7th on Science Hill at the University of California Santa Cruz.
